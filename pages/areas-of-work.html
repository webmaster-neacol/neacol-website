Focus Area

Education

We level the playing field for underprivileged children by providing them with the tools and resources they need to succeed academically and prepare for a brighter future.

Access to quality education is one of the most powerful ways to break the cycle of poverty. NEACOL funds programs that provide tutoring, school supplies, literacy support, vocational training, and after-school programs for children across Colombia.

Our education grants have helped thousands of children stay in school, improve academic performance, and develop critical thinking and life skills needed to thrive.

Health

We help improve the health and well-being of underprivileged children by providing access to healthcare services, preventive care, mental health support, and health education.

This helps to address health disparities and ensure that all children have the opportunity to thrive. Healthy children learn better, grow stronger, and are better equipped to reach their full potential.

NEACOL-funded health programs include medical check-ups, vaccination support, mental health counseling, and community health education initiatives.

See Health Projects
Focus Area

Nutrition

We provide access to nutritious food, address food insecurity, and promote healthy eating habits for Colombian children and their families.

This helps improve children's overall health, reduces the risk of chronic diseases, and helps them develop lifelong healthy habits. Proper nutrition is the foundation for children's physical and cognitive development.

NEACOL nutrition programs support school feeding initiatives, community kitchens, and nutrition education to reduce food insecurity among Colombia's most vulnerable populations.
