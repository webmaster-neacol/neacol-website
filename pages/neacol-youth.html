NEACOL Youth

The Next Generation

Building Future Leaders & Change-Makers

This initiative inspires middle and high-school students to participate in our mission of helping Colombian children while building the next generation of NEACOL leaders and change-makers.

NEACOL Youth supports different programs throughout the year to increase the impact of the work we do, giving young volunteers an opportunity to make a real difference and earn community service hours.
