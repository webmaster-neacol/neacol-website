Volunteer with NEACOL

Why Volunteer?

Make a Real Impact

Our organization is run almost entirely by volunteers. It is through the dedication and hard work of people like you that we are able to make a positive impact on the lives of Colombian children in need.

By volunteering with NEACOL, you have the opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals who share your passion for creating positive change. You will build lasting friendships, develop new skills, and contribute meaningfully to a cause that matters.

Whether you have a background in marketing, finance, events planning, project management, or simply want to help — there is a place for you at NEACOL.

Join a Team

Our Committees

Volunteers can join one or more committees based on their skills and interests.

📣 Marketing

Manage NEACOL's social media presence, create content, design materials, and help spread the word about our mission.

🎉 Events

Plan and coordinate fundraising events, galas, mixers, and other community gatherings that raise funds and awareness.

📊 Projects

Review grant proposals, evaluate applicant organizations, and monitor the impact of funded projects in Colombia.

💰 Fundraising

Develop and execute fundraising strategies, cultivate donor relationships, and secure sponsorships.

💻 IT

Maintain our website, databases, and digital tools to keep our operations running smoothly.

🤝 Membership

Recruit new members, maintain member communications, and grow NEACOL's community of supporters.
