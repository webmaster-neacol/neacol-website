Executive Team Roles

About the Executive Team

NEACOL's executive team handles the day-to-day operations of the organization, supporting both the Board of Directors and the volunteer committees. These roles ensure NEACOL operates efficiently, transparently, and in line with its mission.

Treasurer

Currently: Juan G. Pardo
  • Manages all financial accounts, budgets, and transactions of the organization.
  • Prepares monthly and annual financial statements for the Board.
  • Coordinates with the external CPA for annual tax filings (Form 990) and financial audits.
  • Reviews all grant disbursements and ensures proper documentation.
  • Supports fundraising committees with financial projections and reporting.
  • Maintains compliance with Massachusetts nonprofit regulations and IRS requirements.

Operations Manager

Currently: Natalia Torres
  • Oversees the day-to-day administrative operations of NEACOL.
  • Coordinates communications between the Board, committees, and external partners.
  • Manages organizational records, documentation, and processes.
  • Supports committee chairs with logistics, scheduling, and follow-up.
  • Assists with grant administration, project tracking, and grantee communications.
  • Helps onboard and orient new volunteers to NEACOL's systems and culture.
