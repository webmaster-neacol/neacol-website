2026 Projects

In 2026, NEACOL is continuing its commitment to supporting projects that improve Education. We strive to make a positive impact by diversifying our funding each year and providing valuable resources to local organizations in Colombia. Our focus is on reducing poverty by supporting programs aligned with our areas of interest: Education · Health · Nutrition.
